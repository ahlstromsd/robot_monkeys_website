Purpose of the Robot Monkey

At BYU-I, the Robot Monkey has been introduced to enhance campus safety and provide additional support to our students. Designed with advanced sensors and cameras, it patrols the campus 24/7, monitoring for suspicious activities and potential hazards. Beyond security, it assists students by offering directions, answering questions, and providing quick first aid, ensuring a safer and more convenient campus life.

Reasons why the Robot Monkey is Beneficial

24/7 Vigilance

Constant surveillance and rapid response to incidents.

Quick Emergency Response

Alerts campus security and provides real-time video feeds and basic medical aid.

Student Assistance

Guides students to classes and facilities, and answers common questions.

Promotes Safety

Acts as a deterrent to misconduct and fosters a secure campus enviroment.

Eco-Friendly Operation

Energy-efficient design with minimal enviromental impact.

Enhanced Student Experience

Improves overall campus life by offering support and safety.

Community Trust

Builds trust among students, parents, and faculty.

Cost Effective

Combines surveillance and assistance roles for a cost-effective security solution.

Future-Ready

Prepares BYU-I for future challenges with advanced technology.
